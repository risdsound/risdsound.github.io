25.4 loudspeaker array

The Spatial Audio Studio has a sound system featuring an array of 25 independently-addressable, full-range speakers in a hemisphere configuration, accompanied by 4 subwoofers.Â  A variety of spatial audio techniques are supported, including high order ambisonics, vector-based amplitude panning, multichannel surround, and 3D audio for immersive media.
