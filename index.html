Camille Norment in conversation: October 17

Monday, October 17, 6:30pm
 
Come to the Fleet Library for an evening with celebrated multimedia artist Camille Norment in conversation with Alex Chechile (Fellow, Studio for Research in Sound and Technology) and Jess Myers (Assistant Professor, Architecture Department) co-presented by Fleet Library, Studio for Research in Sound and Technology, and the Architecture Department. Open to all.
